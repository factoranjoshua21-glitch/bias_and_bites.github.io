About Us

Bias and Bites was born from a group of friends with a shared love for great food and good vibes. Our motto, "Pick Your Bias, Enjoy Your Bites", reminds us that everyone has a favorite flavor—just like everyone has a favorite idol. From crafting mouthwatering pizzas to refreshing drinks, we aim to bring joy, friendship, and a little bit of K-pop energy into every bite.
